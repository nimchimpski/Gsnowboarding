ABOUT ME

Live to Ride

Hello,

I'm Gabriel. Nice to meet you!

I was born in France, near the border with Switzerland and Germany, in Alsace.
I started skiing and snowboarding at a young age in French Vosges, and the Swiss and French Alps.

After studying Sport Commerce I decided to head for the mountains, and for many years I lived in the French Alps in a town called Chambery, managing a Snowboard Shop.

After years of dealing with customers I realised I would like to share my skills, knowledge and experience even more - so I decided to train as a Snowboard Instructor

I also practice Wakeboarding and Surfing in the summer.

HOW I TEACH

We find what works best for you

I instruct in German, English and French.

As a snowboard teacher, I love helping people of all skill levels learn and improve.

My lessons are tailored to individual needs and focus on technique and safety.

Whether you're a beginner or experienced rider, I can help you reach your goals.

Come join me on the mountain and let's start your snowboarding journey together!

Lets plan together a personal lesson, if you want to improve your skills, or work on something more specific like:

- Freestyle Shred
- Freestyle Backcountry
- Freeride Backcountry
- Splitboarding....
