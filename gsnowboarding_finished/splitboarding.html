SPLITBOARD DISCOVERY LESSON:
4 HOURS
For the snowboarder who has never tried splitboarding before, with a minimum level of physical fitness.
Splitboard Set ( split + skins + crampon ) + Adjustable hiking poles:

You can rent your splitboard and the safety avalanche gear from various shops in engelberg

Freeride or hiking backpack min 18L with:

  • Obligatory Safety Avalanche Gear:
    • Avalanche Transceiver, probe and shovel.

  • personal kit: food, water, spare layers, camera, etc


LESSON DESCRIPTION:

  • Weather forecast, Risk and Avalanche breifing


  • How to use the Avalanche Safety Gear


  • How to transition the Splitboard between walking and riding mode


  • How to hike up the Mountain


  • How to ride a Splitboard
