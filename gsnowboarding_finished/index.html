Splitboarding

Split and climb - clip together and shred

Splitboarding is an amazing way to experience the mountains.
Instead of riding a lift to the top of the mountain, you hike up using a splitboard.
This allows you to explore the backcountry and ride new terrain.
This makes it a true adventure every time you head out, and a great way to fully immerse yourself in the natural beauty of the Swiss mountain.

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced snowboarder, splitboarding is an exciting and rewarding way to enjoy the backcountry.
